Bangkok Happenings

First I would like to congratulate Bumrungrad International hospital on being named in the Top 10 Hospitals in the world by the people who know these things! You can see the details on the website OnTopLists.com

Bumrungrad and ThaiCraft have been co-organizing Living in Bangkok each year to serve expatriates and their families for decades, it’s not only a great event to attend for fun but to meet people that can help you settle in here, or for old Bangkok hands a chance to see whats new in the community and much more.

Moving to Bangkok, Thailand doesn’t mean you have to give up your favorite interests from your home country. Living in Bangkok 2015 being held this year on Saturday September 19th from 9 am to 5 pm at Bumrungrad International Hospital, Sukhumvit Soi 3. This is a very popular event for expats and their families to connect with the people and organizations that can help make living in Bangkok an enjoyable experience.

There’s entertainment provided by many International schools which you can also meet, lifestyle companies, financial advice, Thai crafts, relocation companies, restaurants and much much more.

Take your time and stroll around the 21st floor Conference Center, Bumrungrad Clinic building. ThaiCraft’s renowned arts and crafts, brought in directly from the Thai villages where they are made, are available for purchase and you can also watch some of the artisans making their products.
